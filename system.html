Daily Screener
How candidates are selected
Every morning before market open, HedgePick's screener evaluates 20,000+ US-listed equities across 9 quantitative signals to surface the highest-conviction setups.
Technical
RSI Recovery
RSI crossed up through oversold territory (below 30) in the last 5 days — classic momentum reversal signal.
Technical
MACD Crossover
MACD line crossed above signal line in the last 3 days — bullish momentum confirmation.
Technical
SMA50 Breakout
Price broke above the 50-day moving average on elevated volume — trend change signal.
Technical
BB Squeeze Breakout
Bollinger Band width compressed then expanded upward — volatility contraction preceding a breakout.
Technical
Volume Surge
Price and volume both accelerating over 5 days vs 20-day baseline — the most reliable precursor to sustained moves.
Fundamental
Revenue Acceleration
Revenue growth rate accelerating quarter-over-quarter — companies growing faster are repriced by the market.
Fundamental
EPS Revision
Analysts revising earnings estimates upward — positive revision cycles tend to persist.
Fundamental
Low PEG Ratio
PEG ratio below 1.0 — growth at a reasonable price relative to sector peers.
Sentiment
Short Squeeze Setup
High short float combined with recent price strength — potential for rapid upside as shorts cover.
