Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

dott.stone LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates HedgePick at hedgepick.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Email address (required for account creation)
  • Name (optional, collected at checkout)
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe — we never store card details)
  • Stock tickers you submit for analysis

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data (analyses run, features used, timestamps)
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Google Analytics data (page views, traffic sources)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Service
  • To send you your analysis reports via email
  • To send transactional emails (account creation, payment confirmation)
  • To send product updates and tips (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To process payments through Stripe
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the product

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • SendGrid — email delivery
  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage analytics
  • Anthropic — AI analysis processing (ticker symbols only, no personal data)

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Analysis reports are stored for 90 days. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure password hashing, and Stripe's PCI-compliant payment processing. We never store credit card numbers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time
  • Request a copy of your data

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We also use Google Analytics cookies for anonymous usage tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at joseph@hedgepick.com
